HOW TO PLAY

Gameplay

The game starts with one player holding the phone.

That player reads one prompt out loud.

Everyone immediately reacts based on the prompt.

After reading, the phone is passed to the next player clockwise.

The next player reads the next prompt and the cycle continues.

Rules

  • No one is forced to drink alcohol.
  • No targeting sensitive or private topics.
  • Respect personal boundaries.
